'HAPPY 2 HELP' is a voluntary organization since 2019.


We do various kinds of social works.


We are providing food, sanitizer, soap,mask etc. to the People of our locality in this covid-19 pandemic situation.

WHAT WE DO?

HEALTH CHECKUP CAMPS

We organise Health checkup camps every year on the occassion of Birthday of Dr. Bidhan Chandra Roy

BLOOD DONATION CAMPS

We organise several Blood donation camps every year on the occasion of Independence day

SCHOLARSHIPS FOR NEEDY STUDENTS

We provide scholarships every year to the poor and meritorious students.
